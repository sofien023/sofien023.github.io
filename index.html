About me

Data-driven by nature, curious by choice

I'm a final-year Computer Science student with a focus on data science and machine learning. I'm passionate about extracting knowledge from complex datasets, building predictive models, and transforming raw numbers into actionable intelligence.

My technical toolkit spans the full data pipeline — from data wrangling and preprocessing with Python and SQL, to building and evaluating machine learning models with scikit-learn, to communicating results through interactive dashboards in Power BI. I also have strong software development skills that let me deploy models into real applications.

Beyond the code, I'm driven by a curiosity about how data shapes decisions — in agriculture, education, business, and beyond. I believe the best data scientists are not just analysts but storytellers who make numbers speak.

"Curious, creative and committed to turning data into decisions that matter."
